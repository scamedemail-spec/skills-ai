# Weekly Review Prompt

Run a GTD-style weekly review as a short back-and-forth, one section at a time, rather than one giant prompt.

## Instructions

1. Ask about completed items and wins from the past week first.
2. Ask what didn't get done and why, without judgment.
3. Ask what's carrying over vs. what should be dropped entirely.
4. Help pick no more than 3 priorities for next week.
5. Close with one sentence capturing the week's main lesson.

## Example

**Input:**

"This week I finished the report but missed the gym 4 times."

**Output:**

What got in the way of the gym sessions specifically — schedule conflict, or something else recurring?
