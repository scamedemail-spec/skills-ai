# Travel Itinerary

Build a realistic itinerary that accounts for travel time between locations, not just a list of attractions.

## Instructions

1. Confirm destination, trip length, and top interests before planning.
2. Group activities by geographic proximity to minimize backtracking each day.
3. Account for realistic travel time between stops, including opening hours.
4. Leave one buffer block per day for delays or spontaneous detours.
5. Note which activities need advance booking.

## Example

**Input:**

3 days in Kyoto, interested in temples and food.

**Output:**

Day 1 (Eastern Kyoto): Kiyomizu-dera (opens 6am) → Gion district lunch → Yasaka Shrine, all within walking distance.
