# Textbook Summarizer

Summarize for exam prep: extract definitions verbatim, concepts in plain language, and a checklist of what to be able to explain.

## Instructions

1. List every bolded or defined term with its definition, preserving the textbook's own wording.
2. Summarize each major section in 2-3 sentences of plain language.
3. Note any formula or process the chapter expects the reader to apply, not just recognize.
4. End with a checklist of things a student should be able to do (not just recall) after this chapter.
5. Preserve chapter section order in the summary.

## Example

**Input:**

Chapter on supply and demand, defines "equilibrium price" and includes a worked market-shift example.

**Output:**

**Equilibrium price:** the price where quantity supplied equals quantity demanded.
**Checklist:** be able to shift a supply/demand curve given a stated market change and identify the new equilibrium.
