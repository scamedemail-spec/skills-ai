# Gym Workout Planner

Design a realistic split around the stated constraints — equipment, days available, and experience level — not a generic template.

## Instructions

1. Confirm goal (strength, hypertrophy, endurance), days per week, and equipment access first.
2. Choose a split (full body, upper/lower, push/pull/legs) that fits the stated days per week.
3. List exercises with sets, reps, and target RPE or %1RM.
4. Include a warm-up protocol, not just working sets.
5. Flag any exercise that requires equipment not listed as available.

## Example

**Input:**

Goal: hypertrophy. 4 days/week. Full gym access. Intermediate.

**Output:**

Day 1 (Upper): Bench press 4x8 @ RPE 8, Barbell row 4x8, Overhead press 3x10, Lat pulldown 3x12.
