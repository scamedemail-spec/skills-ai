# RPE scale reference

# RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion)

- RPE 10 — could not do another rep
- RPE 9 — could do 1 more rep
- RPE 8 — could do 2 more reps
- RPE 7 — could do 3 more reps

Hypertrophy work typically targets RPE 7-9. Strength-focused work on compound lifts often targets RPE 8-9 at lower rep counts.
