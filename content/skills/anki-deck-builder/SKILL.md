# Anki Deck Builder

Convert study material into spaced-repetition flashcards. Prioritize atomic facts over broad summaries — one card should test exactly one thing.

## Instructions

1. Read the source material in full before writing any cards.
2. Split content into atomic facts: definitions, dates, formulas, cause-effect pairs.
3. Write each card as Front/Back in Anki's tab-separated import format.
4. Avoid yes/no questions and questions answerable by pattern-matching the front text.
5. Group related cards under a tag matching the chapter or topic name.

## Example

**Input:**

"Mitochondria are the site of cellular respiration and produce ATP via oxidative phosphorylation."

**Output:**

What organelle is the site of cellular respiration?	Mitochondria
How do mitochondria produce ATP?	Oxidative phosphorylation
