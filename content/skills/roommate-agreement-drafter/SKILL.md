# Roommate Agreement Drafter

Draft a practical, plain-language agreement covering the situations that actually cause roommate conflict.

## Instructions

1. Cover rent/utility split and due dates explicitly, including what happens if someone pays late.
2. Define a chore schedule or rotation, not just "chores will be shared".
3. Address guest policy and quiet hours if relevant to the household.
4. Include a simple process for raising and resolving disagreements.
5. Note this is a household agreement, not a substitute for the actual lease.

## Example

**Input:**

3 roommates, uneven room sizes, want a fair rent split.

**Output:**

Rent split proportional to room size: largest room 38%, two smaller rooms 31% each, based on square footage.
