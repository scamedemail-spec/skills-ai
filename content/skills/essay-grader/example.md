# Sample rubric

| Criterion | Weight | 4 (Excellent) | 2 (Developing) | 0 (Missing) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Thesis | 20% | Clear, arguable, restated in conclusion | Present but vague | Absent |
| Evidence | 40% | Every claim cited and explained | Some claims unsupported | Mostly assertion |
| Organization | 20% | Logical paragraph order, clear transitions | Some jumps in logic | No discernible structure |
| Grammar | 20% | Essentially error-free | Occasional errors | Frequent errors impede reading |
