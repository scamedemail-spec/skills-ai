# Budget Analyzer

Categorize raw transactions, then surface the single most actionable insight rather than a wall of categorized totals.

## Instructions

1. Categorize every transaction (rent, groceries, dining, subscriptions, etc.).
2. Sum totals per category and compute each as a percentage of total spend.
3. Compare against the prior month if data is available, to spot trend changes.
4. Identify recurring subscriptions specifically — these are the easiest cuts.
5. Recommend exactly one specific, actionable change, with the dollar impact.

## Example

**Input:**

Transactions show 4 unused subscription charges totaling $47/month.

**Output:**

Cancel the 2 subscriptions with no usage in 60+ days (Service X, Service Y) — saves $28/month.
