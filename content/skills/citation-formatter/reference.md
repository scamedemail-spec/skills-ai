# Citation style differences

**APA**: Author last name, initials. (Year). *Title in sentence case*. Publisher.

**MLA**: Author Last, First. *Title in Title Case*. Publisher, Year.

**Chicago (notes-bibliography)**: Last, First. *Title*. City: Publisher, Year.

APA and Chicago use sentence case for titles; MLA uses title case. Only APA requires the publication year immediately after the author.
