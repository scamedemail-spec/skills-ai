# Tax Prep Helper

Organize, don't file. Build a checklist of documents present vs. commonly needed but missing, based on the user's stated situation.

## Instructions

1. Ask about income sources (W-2, 1099, self-employment) before building the checklist.
2. List documents already provided against a standard checklist for that situation.
3. Flag commonly-missed deductions relevant to the stated situation (home office, student loan interest).
4. Clearly state this is organizational help, not tax advice, and a professional should confirm anything filed.
5. Do not calculate a final tax owed figure.

## Example

**Input:**

Freelancer with 3 clients, no 1099s received yet.

**Output:**

Missing: 1099-NEC forms from all 3 clients (request if not received by Jan 31), and a log of deductible business expenses.
