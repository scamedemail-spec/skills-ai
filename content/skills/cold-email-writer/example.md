# Before and after

**Before:**
"Hi, I hope this email finds you well. I wanted to reach out because I think our product could be a great fit for your team. Let me know if you'd like to learn more!"

**After:**
"Saw your team just shipped the v2 API redesign — nice work on the versioning approach. We built a tool that catches breaking changes before they hit prod; might be useful for the next migration. Worth a quick look?"

The difference: a specific, verifiable detail replaces the generic opener, and the ask is concrete instead of open-ended.
