# Thesis Outline

Structure a thesis around the research question, ensuring every chapter earns its place in answering it.

## Instructions

1. Restate the research question precisely before outlining anything.
2. Draft chapter titles that map directly to stages of answering the question (context, method, findings, discussion).
3. Under each chapter, list 2-4 subsections describing their specific content, not generic labels.
4. Flag any chapter that doesn't clearly connect back to the research question.
5. Note where existing literature vs. original contribution belongs in the structure.

## Example

**Input:**

Research question: "Does remote work reduce team communication quality in software teams?"

**Output:**

Ch. 3 — Methodology: survey design measuring communication quality across 40 remote and 40 co-located teams, instrument validation, sampling approach.
