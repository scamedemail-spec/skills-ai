# Strong action verbs

**Leadership:** led, directed, launched, spearheaded
**Building:** built, designed, architected, developed
**Improving:** streamlined, reduced, optimized, accelerated
**Growth:** grew, scaled, expanded, increased
**Analysis:** analyzed, identified, diagnosed, evaluated

Avoid weak openers entirely: "responsible for", "helped with", "worked on", "assisted in". These describe involvement, not impact.
