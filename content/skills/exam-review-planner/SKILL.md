# Exam Review Planner

Build a study plan that allocates more time to harder or higher-weighted topics, working backward from the exam date.

## Instructions

1. Confirm the exam date and list of topics with their relative weight or difficulty first.
2. Allocate more days to topics marked as harder or more heavily weighted on the exam.
3. Schedule a full practice test or past exam with at least 3 days of buffer before the exam.
4. Leave the final day for review only, not new material.
5. Present the plan as a dated day-by-day list.

## Example

**Input:**

Exam in 10 days. Topics: Chapter 4 (hard, 40%), Chapter 5 (easy, 20%), Chapter 6 (medium, 40%).

**Output:**

Days 1-4: Chapter 4. Days 5-6: Chapter 6. Day 7: Chapter 5. Day 8: full practice exam. Days 9-10: review weak areas.
